2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. Cookies are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide reporting information.

We use cookies for several reasons:

  • Performance cookies: Collect information about how visitors use our website.
  • Functionality cookies: Remember user preferences to enhance experience.
  • Third-party cookies: Set by external services (e.g., analytics tools or embedded videos).

5. How Can You Control Cookies?

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. You can also opt-out of some third-party cookies using tools provided by the Network Advertising Initiative or Digital Advertising Alliance.
